Eminem a.k.a. Slim Shady

Marshall Bruce Mathers III was born on October 17, 1972, to a fifteen-year-old mother, in Saint. Joseph Missouri.

His life was always in upheaval due to moving back and forth from Missouri to Michigan.

He struggled in school with bullies and grades. After failing the 9th grade three times, he dropped out at the age of 17.

Despite all of the turmoil in his life, he had an affinity for English and would study the dictionary rigorously.

This knowledge helped propel him into rap music. A journey that has spanned decades, won him countless accolades and awards, and enabled him to become one of the greatest rappers of all time.
